ANGSTROM — Data & Privacy Notice
🗄️
All data is stored locally on your device only. ANGSTROM has no server, no account, and no cloud sync. Nothing you enter is ever sent anywhere.
⚠️
Data loss risk is real. Clearing browser cache, switching browsers, or resetting your device will permanently erase all your questions. There is no recovery.
🔄
Back up regularly. Export your data as JSON often (Settings → Export JSON) and store it somewhere safe — Google Drive, USB, or email it to yourself.
📁
For safer storage, go to Settings → Device Storage and connect a local folder. Your data will be saved as real files you control directly.
🔒
This is a personal tool. Each person who opens this app gets their own completely private, isolated journal — data is never shared between users.
You can re-read this notice anytime in Settings → Read Before Using.
